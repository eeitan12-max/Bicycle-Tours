About This Tour

The Netherlands invented cycling culture, and this 5-day tour is your invitation to experience it at its finest. The Dutch cycle infrastructure is unmatched anywhere in the world — dedicated cycle highways connect every city, town, and village, keeping you safe and separated from traffic at all times. On this route you'll tick off everything the Netherlands is famous for: windmills, tulip fields, canals, Delftware pottery, stroopwafels, and some of the most beautiful old city centres in Europe.

The terrain is famously flat, making this the perfect tour for beginners, families, or anyone who simply wants to enjoy the scenery without worrying about the gradient. Spring departures pass through the Keukenhof bulb fields at peak bloom, when the countryside explodes in blocks of red, yellow, and purple. The tour ends in Rotterdam — Europe's largest port and one of its most exciting modern cities.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Amsterdam

Meet at Amsterdam Centraal station. Bike fitting and a guided spin through the historic canal ring to your hotel in the Jordaan neighbourhood. Welcome dinner at a traditional Dutch eetcafé. Distance: 8 km.

Day 2

Amsterdam → Haarlem via Tulip Fields

Head west through the Haarlemmermeer polder, cycling past kilometre after kilometre of tulip fields in full spring colour. Visit the Keukenhof gardens (April–May), the world's largest flower garden. Arrive in elegant Haarlem for the night. Distance: 42 km.

Day 3

Haarlem → Leiden along the Dunes

Cycle south along the North Sea dune coast on smooth cycle paths through the National Park Zuid-Kennemerland. Stop at the beach village of Zandvoort, then head inland to the beautiful university city of Leiden, birthplace of Rembrandt. Distance: 48 km.

Day 4

Leiden → Delft via The Hague

Ride through The Hague (Den Haag), the Dutch seat of government, past the Binnenhof and the Peace Palace. Continue south to Delft — one of the most perfectly preserved 17th-century Dutch cities, and home of the famous blue-and-white pottery. Distance: 45 km.

Day 5

Delft → Rotterdam — Departure

A short final ride into the futuristic skyline of Rotterdam. Visit the iconic Cube Houses and the Markthal, then farewells at Rotterdam Centraal. Trains to Schiphol Airport run every 15 minutes. Distance: 18 km.
